## Upgrade Notes

Existing repositories do not need to re-index solely to adopt this version.
After upgrading the CTX Layer package, run:

```powershell
ctxlayer --repo . doctor
ctxlayer --repo . workflow lint --summary
ctxlayer --repo . flywheel status
```

For large existing workspaces, back up `.ctxlayer/workspace.db` first and keep
the storage maintenance checks from `0.2.0a11` in place:

```powershell
ctxlayer --repo . storage-report
ctxlayer --repo . retention show
ctxlayer --repo . gc --deep --dry-run
```
