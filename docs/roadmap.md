# Roadmap

The roadmap is ordered by qualification risk, not feature count.

## Release recovery and qualification

- Maintain one frozen candidate per version.
- Keep source, build, installed-wheel, storage, recovery, and publication
  evidence tied to that candidate.
- Classify requirements so unresolved beta blockers reach zero.

## Closed beta

- Restrict operation to core-local repositories and manual review.
- Enrol a small set of Windows 10/11 design partners.
- Measure installation, indexing, search, pack usefulness, workflow completion,
  storage failures, verification accuracy, corrections, and recovery events.
- Run a multi-week reliability soak.

## Team and public beta

- Qualify identity, remote authorization, team HTTP, controlled mutation,
  multi-user concurrency, security review, and deployment soak.

## Stable and enterprise

- Establish monitoring, disaster-recovery exercises, support and compatibility
  policies, long-duration soak, and formal production approval.

Advanced intelligence capabilities remain evidence-gated and are promoted
individually only after genuine usage demonstrates benefit and safe rollback.
