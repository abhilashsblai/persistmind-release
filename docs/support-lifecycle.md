# Support Lifecycle

## Current policy

Internal previews are short-lived evaluation builds. They receive no guaranteed
security-fix window, compatibility SLA, or production support. A newer preview
may supersede an older one immediately, but its release record remains available
for provenance.

Closed beta, public beta, stable, and LTS windows will be published before those
channels open. Each supported line will identify:

- first supported date;
- supported platforms and Python versions;
- maintenance and security-fix policy;
- allowed upgrade and rollback paths;
- deprecation notice period; and
- end-of-support date.

Absence of a published window means no support commitment should be inferred.
