# Release Support Policy

## Internal Preview

Internal previews are short-lived evaluation builds. They have no production,
enterprise, compatibility, security-fix, or response-time SLA. A newer preview
may supersede an older one immediately.

## Future channels

Closed Beta, Public Beta, Stable, and LTS support windows will be published
before those channels open. Each supported line must identify:

- first supported date;
- supported operating systems and Python versions;
- maintenance and security-fix policy;
- supported upgrade and rollback paths;
- deprecation notice period; and
- end-of-support date.

Absence of a published window means no support commitment should be inferred.
See [SUPPORT.md](../SUPPORT.md) for issue-reporting requirements.
